Medieval Salmon

Image by Karina Panchenko

It's not luck, it has always been salmon - Unknown Medieval Survivor

To some, it is called a mana potion, but to the wise, it is where the energy of the world flourishes. A perfect meal for restoring holistic energy.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Salmon Fillet
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper

How to prepare

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub the salmon with olive oil, place salt and pepper over the fillet.
  3. Bake on the grill for about 5 minutes each side, the fish is ready when it flakes apart with a simple pass of a fork
