Medieval Wings

Image by Pascal Wicht

Don't put away for tomorrow the barbecue that you can do today - Unknown Medieval Savant

Tasty BBQ medieval wings made from the most torrid ember to invigorate the best combatants in the kingdom.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings (enough to satiate the realm)
  • Salt (to your taste)
  • Pepper (from the spice trade)
  • Basil (collected on the most suitable soils)

How to prepare

  1. Place the chicken wings in a large bowl.
  2. Place salt, pepper and chopped basil over the chicken wings.
  3. Bake on the grill for about 45 minutes, flipping halfway until skin is golden brown and crispy.
