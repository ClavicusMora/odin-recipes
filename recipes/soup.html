Medieval Soup

Image by Rawpixel

Don't even come with a fork, it's soup day - Unknown Medieval Genius

Soup made with the best vegetables that the garden of life can provide, strengthening the health even for the weakest.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Salt (to your taste)
  • Water

How to prepare

  1. Dice all vegetables.
  2. Place the vegetables in a pot, add water, salt, turn on the stove and wait until boiling.
  3. Serve and be careful not to burn yourself.
